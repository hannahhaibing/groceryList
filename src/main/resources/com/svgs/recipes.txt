Classic Chicken Caesar Salad*
Arrange lettuce on four serving plates. Top with chicken, Parmesan, and croutons, then drizzle with dressing.*
6 cups tightly packed chopped romaine lettuce,1 pound cooked boneless and skinless chicken breasts cut into strips,1/2 cup finely shredded Parmesan cheese,1/2 cup seasoned croutons,1/4 cup creamy Caesar dressing***
Chicken Broccoli Rice Casserole*
Preheat oven to 375 F. Spray a 9 x 13-inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray. In the prepared dish, whisk together the cream of chicken soup and the broth or water. Stir in the uncooked rice, the uncooked chicken, and the frozen broccoli. Season with about 1/2 teaspoon salt and 1/4 teaspoon pepper. Stir again so that everything is well combined. Cover tightly with foil and bake for 55-60 minutes. Use a fork to fluff the rice. Sprinkle the cheese on top of the dish. Return to the oven until the cheese melts.*
1 (10.5 oz) can condensed cream of chicken soup,2 cups chicken broth or water or milk,1 cup uncooked long grain white rice,1 lb. uncooked boneless and skinless diced chicken breasts,1 (12.6 oz) bag frozen baby broccoli florets,1 cup grated sharp cheddar cheese,Herbs to taste***
Baked Feta Pasta*
Preheat oven to 400 F. In a 13x9-in. baking dish, combine tomatoes, garlic and 1/4 cup olive oil. Place the block of feta in the center, moving tomatoes so the cheese is sitting on the bottom of the dish. Drizzle the feta with remaining oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Bake until tomato skins start to split and garlic has softened, 30-40 minutes. Cook pasta for al dente. Drain, reserving 1 cup pasta water. Stir the feta mixture, lightly pressing tomatoes, until combined. Add pasta, stir in reserved pasta water, and sprinkle with basil.*
2 pints cherry tomatoes,3 halved garlic cloves,1/2 divided cup olive oil,1 package (8 ounces) block feta cheese,1 teaspoon sea salt,1/4 teaspoon coarsely ground pepper,1 package (16 ounces) rigatoni,Fresh coarsely chopped basil leaves***
Shrimp Quesadilla*
Heat 2 teaspoons oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat; add onion and jalapeno. Cook and stir until tender, 3-5 minutes. Add shrimp, cumin and pepper; cook and stir until shrimp turn pink, 2-3 minutes. Transfer to a small bowl; stir in tomato. Add 1 teaspoon oil to the same skillet; add 1 tortilla. Top with half the cheese, half the shrimp mixture and 1 tortilla. Cook over medium heat until lightly browned, 2-3 minutes on each side; remove. Repeat with remaining 1 teaspoon oil, tortillas, cheese and shrimp mixture. Cut into wedges.*
4 tsps divided canola oil,1/4 cup chopped onion,1 tbsp finely chopped jalapeno pepper,1/2 lb uncooked and peeled and chopped and deveined shrimp,1/4 tsp ground cumin,1/8 tsp pepper,1/4 cup chopped tomato,4 flour tortillas (6 inches),1/2 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend***
Chicken Spinach Pasta*
Cook pasta according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a Dutch oven over medium heat, cook and stir chicken and onion in oil for 5 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink. Stir in soup and cream. Bring to a boil over medium heat. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, for 2 minutes. Stir in the spinach, cheese and pepper; cook for 1-2 minutes or until spinach is wilted and cheese is melted. Drain pasta; add to chicken mixture and toss to coat. If desired, sprinkle with red pepper flakes and Parmesan.*
1-1/2 cups uncooked penne pasta,1 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts cut into cubes,1/2 cup chopped onion,2 tsps olive oil,1 can (10-3/4 ounces) condensed cream of mushroom soup,1 cup heavy whipping cream,10 cups chopped spinach,2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese,1/8 tsp pepper***
Slow-Cooker Spareribs*
Place ribs in a 5-qt. slow cooker. Combine the remaining ingredients; pour over ribs. Cover and cook on low for 5-6 hours or until meat is tender.*
4 pounds pork spareribs cut into serving-size pieces,1/4 cup soy sauce,1/4 cup prepared mustard,1/4 cup molasses,3 tablespoons cider vinegar,2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce,1 to 2 teaspoons hot pepper sauce***

Shrimp with Orzo and Feta*
Cook orzo according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a large skillet, heat oil over medium heat. Add garlic; cook and stir 1 minute. Add tomatoes and lemon juice. Bring to a boil. Stir in shrimp. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, until shrimp turn pink, 4-5 minutes. Drain orzo. Add orzo, cilantro and pepper to shrimp mixture; heat through. Sprinkle with feta cheese.*
1-1/4 cups uncooked whole wheat orzo pasta,2 tbsps olive oil,2 minced garlic cloves,2 medium chopped tomatoes,2 tbsps lemon juice,1-1/4 pounds uncooked and peeled and deveined shrimp peeled,2 tbsps minced fresh cilantro,1/4 tsp pepper,1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese***
Easy Beef and Noodles*
Cook noodles according to package directions. In a large skillet, saute mushrooms and onion in oil until tender. Add broth, roast beef and pepper. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes. Drain noodles; stir into skillet. If desired, top with sour cream and parsley.*
2-1/2 cups uncooked yolk-free noodles,1/3 cup sliced fresh mushrooms,1/3 cup chopped onion,1 tablespoon olive oil,1-1/4 cups reduced-sodium beef broth,6 ounces deli roast beef cubed,1/8 teaspoon pepper,Optional: Sour cream and minced fresh parsley***
